# FactoryOS

A full-stack ERP web application built to manage the daily operations of a steel manufacturing factory. It handles everything from raw material inventory and production tracking to sales, HR, and contractor payrolls.

## Tech Stack
* **Frontend:** React.js, TailwindCSS
* **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
* **Database:** PostgreSQL

## What it does

* **Role-Based Access:** Secure login system with different access levels. Ensures that users only see the data they are supposed to.
* **Purchase & Sales:** Manages the buying of raw materials and selling of finished goods. It also auto-calculates GST and generates instant, print-ready invoices.
* **Production Tracking:** Keeps track of the manufacturing process, updating the inventory as raw materials are converted into finished products.
* **HR & Payroll:** A complete system to manage daily staff attendance, process monthly salaries, and keep track of advance payments.
* **Contractor Management:** A custom payroll module specifically for contractors. It automatically calculates their pay based on the exact number of items they produce.
* **Finance & Expenses:** Tracks daily business expenses by category and manages pending settlements with different vendors and parties.
